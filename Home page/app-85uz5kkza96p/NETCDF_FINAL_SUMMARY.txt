SOLUTION:

  2. Ensure data variable has 2+ dimensions
  3. Avoid names like time, depth, level

SOLUTION:
  1. Extract subset: ncks -d lat,20,40 input.nc output.nc
  2. Reduce resolution: cdo remapbil,r180x90 input.nc output.nc
  3. Extract variable: ncks -v temperature input.nc output.nc
